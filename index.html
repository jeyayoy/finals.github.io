About Me

Hi! I am Jessella, a college student student from Occidental Mindoro State College San Jose Campus and I am delighted to share my portfolio with you.

Who I am

My name is Jessella Marie V. Cajayon, a 21-year-old from Burgos Street, Barangay Poblacion 2, San Jose, Occidental Mindoro.





A Glimpse into My World

My journey in life is deeply rooted in my love for creativity, self-expression, and storytelling. From a young age, I have always found solace and inspiration in activities that allow me to bring my imagination to life, wether through strokes of a pencil, the melodies of a song, or the enchanting stories of anime and manga. Each of my hobbies reflects a unique facet of my personality and my passion for discovering beauty in every form.

What I'm good at?

Singing

Singing is more than just a hobby for me; it's a way of life. It allows me to express emotions that often go unspoken. It makes me feel alive and connected to something greater than myself. Music has an incredible power to heal, inspire, and bring people together, and I cherish the moment's when my voice touch others' hearts. Singing reminds me that even the simplest melodies can tell profound stories.

Musical Instruments

Another one of my creative outlets is playing musical instruments. Wether it's strumming the strings of a guitar, pressing keys on a piano, or exploring other instruments, I find joy in creating harmonies and rythms. Playing instruments challenges me to blend technical skill with emotion, giving life to melodies that can evoke a spectrum of feelings. It's a deeply satisfying experience that allows me to explore music on a different level while sharpening my discipline and patience.

Art

Drawing, on the other hand, is my way of capturing the world around me as I see it. With every stroke of a pencil or brush, I can turn blank canvases into vibrant creations that reflect my imagination and emotions. Drawing allows me to express ideas that words cannot fully articulate. I enjoy experimenting with Different styles, from intricate anime-inspired characters to semi-realistic portraits. It's a meditative process that not only hones my skills but also deepens my understanding of visual storytelling.

Make up

Make up is another artistic expression that I've come to love. For me, make up is not just about enhancing physical appearance but also about the freedom to express my personality and creativity. Experimenting with colors, textures, and techniques allows me to create unique looks that reflect my mood and style. It's a medium through which I can transform myself, wether into a bold and confident persona or a soft and understated version of me. Beyond artistry, make up empowers me by boosting my confidence and reminding me of the importance of self-care.
